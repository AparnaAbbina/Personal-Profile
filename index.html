    About Me


    I am an enthusiastic junior software developer who has recently completed a rigorous 16-week boot camp at The School of Code. Throughout this program, I have acquired an extensive understanding of full-stack web development. My enthusiasm for problem-solving, programming, logical analysis, and innovative thinking has been a driving force in my career.

    During my academic tenure in Electronics and Communication Engineering, I developed a profound interest in coding, quickly becoming a subject of great interest to me. I honed my programming abilities through self-directed learning in various programming languages and subsequently served as a software engineer for eighteen months at Neureol Technologies in India. Following my relocation to the UK with my Husband, I devoted seven years to nurturing our young children at home. In due course, I ventured into entrepreneurship with a handicraft business, turning a personal hobby into a successful enterprise.

    I am very happy with my accomplishments and eager to reignite my love for coding and ongoing learning, which I find extremely exciting. This desire led me to pursue additional education at the School of Code, where I improved my skills to contribute to the creation of a more inclusive and empowering internet. I am prepared to join a mission-driven organization where I can use my collaborative spirit and creativity to solve intricate problems!
